About us

Thee Attic is a unique and sustainable secondhand clothing store that is revolutionizing the way people think about fashion. We are dedicated to finding high-quality secondhand clothing and offering it to our customers at a stable and affordable price. Our goal is to create an inclusive shopping experience that is open to everyone, regardless of size, age, aesthetic, or income level.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and our promise to always carry affordable clothing that caters to a low carbon footprint lifestyle. As a partnership business run by two passionate women entrepreneurs, we are committed to bringing our vision to life and making a positive impact in the community.

To make our products accessible to everyone, we plan to start our sales at markets around the Western Cape and advertise our online store, which is equipped with courier services nationwide. As we continue to grow and expand our reach, we plan to find a central location to open our first store, which will be the first of many locations to come.

Join us in our mission to change the world one garment at a time!
